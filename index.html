Welcome to my Travel Blog

From April-September 2018, I will be completing a 4.5-month adventure around the Pacific Rim and I want to document and share my journey!

Beginning in April, I will be attending the International Monetary Fund & World Bank Civil Society Organizations Spring Meetings in Washington D.C. This will be followed by a week of further exploring the D.C., Virginia and Baltimore area before flying cross-country to explore San Fransisco and the Bay Area. From May-June, I will be travelling to China to complete a short-term study abroad to learn about the Chinese language and culture. July has me travelling to Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, Malaysia, the Maldives and Singapore. Finally, in August I’ll be returning to Canada, and exploring the West Coast travelling around British Columbia and Alberta. Topping this adventure off is a bachelorette party in Collingwood, Ontario before finally returning home to my friends and family in Kitchener-Waterloo.

I hope you enjoy following me on my journey this summer and I can’t wait to share my experience and interact with you!
